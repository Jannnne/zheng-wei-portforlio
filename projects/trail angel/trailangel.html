Trail Angel

overview

Trail Angel is a mobile app that assists active people to plan adventures outdoors without worrying about air pollution due to wildfires. The app includes connection to a portable air tracker to monitor conditions real-time. Trail Angel also provides alternative route planning should an area become unsafe, so outdoor activities can still be enjoyed safely.
